What you can do
This week · +$1,800 /mo

Stop buying ingredients you don't use. Food cost is 34% of revenue. Industry benchmark is 28–30%. That gap is $1,800/mo going straight to waste.

How to do it
1Pull your last 4 weeks of invoices and compare to POS sales. Identify which items you over-ordered.
2Set par levels for your top 10 ingredients based on weekly sales, not habit.
3Order to par for 30 days. Measure food cost at month end. Target: under 30%.

What to watch
Running out of an item
86ing one item for a day is cheaper than $1,800/mo in waste.
Staff ordering by habit
One person controls orders, one number, every week — or the system breaks.
Over-correcting
Don't cut so tight you run out. Par levels, not zero stock.
